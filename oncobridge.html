About Us

Where Clinical Expertise Meets Regulatory Excellence

OncoBridge Health Solutions is a pioneering oncology pharmacy and regulatory consultancy dedicated to bridging gaps in cancer care across Kenya and beyond.

"Our mission is to empower healthcare providers, researchers, and patients with innovative solutions that enhance treatment outcomes and patient safety — integrating clinical expertise with regulatory excellence to drive progress in oncology care."

What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive oncology pharmacy and regulatory solutions tailored to your unique needs.

💊

Oncology Pharmacotherapy

Expert clinical guidance on cancer treatment regimens, drug interactions, dose optimization, and evidence-based pharmacotherapy protocols for improved patient outcomes.

📋

Clinical Trial Documentation

Comprehensive support in designing, preparing, and managing clinical trial documentation — from protocol development to regulatory submission packages.

🏛️

Regulatory Affairs

Strategic regulatory consultancy for pharmaceutical products, ensuring full compliance with Kenya's PPB and international standards including EMA and FDA guidelines.

🔬

Research & Evidence Synthesis

Translating the latest oncology research into actionable clinical insights — systematic reviews, literature analysis, and evidence-based treatment recommendations.

🎓

Training & Capacity Building

Structured education programs for healthcare professionals, equipping oncology teams with updated knowledge in pharmacology, safety monitoring, and compliance.

🛡️

Patient Safety & Pharmacovigilance

Robust pharmacovigilance frameworks and patient safety monitoring systems to identify, evaluate, and prevent adverse drug reactions in oncology settings.
